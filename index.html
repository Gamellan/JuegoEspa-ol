El Pueblo Encantado
A Spanish Learning Adventure · Spain Edition
A curse has silenced the village of Aldea Verborum. Its inhabitants can no longer speak correctly. Only a language adventurer can restore their voices — one correct word at a time.
Choose your level

Greetings, numbers, colours, basic nouns. Match objects to their Spanish names.

The Subjunctive Curse

Master the Subjunctive to defeat the final boss and break the curse forever.

🎮 How to Play

🗺️ Town Map — Visit locations like the café, market, or train station. Each has quests and NPCs waiting for you.

⚔️ Dialogue Combat — Face language monsters! Answer grammar questions before the timer runs out. Wrong answers cost HP.

🎒 Word Inventory — Collect Spanish words as loot. Combine articles + nouns (e.g. "el" + "sol") to unlock secrets.

📈 Progression — Complete quests to earn XP. Level up from A1 to B2 and defeat the final boss!

💡 Grammar Notes — Every answer comes with an explanation so you always learn, win or lose.
